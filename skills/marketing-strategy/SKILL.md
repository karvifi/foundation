# SKILL: Marketing Strategy

## Purpose
Build a marketing system that consistently attracts, converts, and retains the right customers.

## Process

### Step 1: Market & Customer Definition
- Who is the exact ideal customer? (not "everyone")
- What specific pain do they have?
- Where do they spend time online/offline?
- What language do they use to describe their problem?
- What have they tried before that did not work?

### Step 2: Positioning Statement
```
For [target customer]
Who [has this specific problem]
[Product/Service] is a [category]
That [primary benefit]
Unlike [competitor],
We [key differentiator]
```

### Step 3: Messaging Hierarchy
- **Headline:** What transformation do you offer? (outcome-focused)
- **Sub-headline:** How? (mechanism or approach)
- **3 Key Benefits:** What specifically do they get?
- **Social Proof:** Who has done it? What results?
- **CTA:** What is the exact next step?

### Step 4: Channel Selection (pick 2-3, not 10)
Score each channel by: Audience match / Cost / Speed / Scalability

| Channel | Best for | Time to results |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| SEO/Content | Long-term organic traffic | 3-6 months |
| Paid Search (Google) | High-intent buyers | Days |
| Paid Social (Meta/IG) | Awareness + retargeting | Days |
| Email marketing | Nurture + retention | Immediate |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional audience | Weeks |
| YouTube | Education + trust building | Months |
| Partnerships/Affiliates | Low CAC at scale | Months |
| Product-led growth | SaaS self-serve | Immediate |
| PR/Media | Credibility + reach | Weeks-months |

### Step 5: Funnel Design
```
AWARENESS → How do strangers find you?
[Content? Ads? Word of mouth? SEO? Events?]

INTEREST → How do you capture attention?
[Blog, video, lead magnet, free tool?]

CONSIDERATION → How do you build trust?
[Case studies, demos, trials, email sequence?]

INTENT → What converts a prospect?
[Offer, urgency, guarantee, social proof?]

CONVERSION → What is the exact purchase path?
[Landing page → checkout → onboarding]

RETENTION → How do you keep them?
[Onboarding, support, community, updates?]

REFERRAL → How do they bring others?
[Referral program, reviews, case studies?]
```

### Step 6: Content Calendar (90-day)
- 4 content pillars (themes that serve your audience)
- Weekly publication cadence
- Content-to-promotion ratio: 80% value, 20% promotion
- Repurposing plan: one piece → multiple formats

### Step 7: Metrics Dashboard
Track weekly:
- Traffic (by channel)
- Leads generated
- Conversion rate (visit → lead, lead → customer)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC ratio (must be > 3:1 to be sustainable)

## Output
- Positioning statement
- Messaging hierarchy
- 2-3 priority channels with execution plan
- Funnel map with conversion targets
- 90-day content calendar
- Metrics dashboard template

## Quality checks
- [ ] Ideal customer is specific (not a demographic, a person)
- [ ] Positioning differentiates from top 3 competitors
- [ ] No more than 3 channels (focus > coverage)
- [ ] Funnel has a clear path at every stage
- [ ] Metrics defined before campaign starts
