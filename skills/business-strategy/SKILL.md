# SKILL: Business Strategy

## Purpose
Design a business strategy that is coherent, executable, and measurable — not just a vision.

## Process

### Step 1: Business Model Design (using Business Model Canvas)
Answer each block:
1. **Customer Segments** — Exactly who? (be specific)
2. **Value Propositions** — What specific problem solved? What gain created?
3. **Channels** — How do customers find, buy, receive value?
4. **Customer Relationships** — How is the relationship maintained?
5. **Revenue Streams** — How does money flow in? What do customers pay for?
6. **Key Resources** — What is essential to deliver the value?
7. **Key Activities** — What must be done to deliver the value?
8. **Key Partnerships** — Who do you need? What do you outsource?
9. **Cost Structure** — What are the main costs?

### Step 2: Competitive Positioning
Porter's Five Forces assessment:
- Competitive rivalry (intensity)
- Supplier power
- Buyer power
- Threat of new entrants
- Threat of substitutes

Positioning strategy (choose ONE):
- Cost leadership (cheapest)
- Differentiation (most unique)
- Focus/niche (specific segment)

### Step 3: Growth Strategy
Ansoff Matrix:
- Market penetration (existing product, existing market) — lowest risk
- Market development (existing product, new market)
- Product development (new product, existing market)
- Diversification (new product, new market) — highest risk

### Step 4: Financial Model (unit economics first)
Key metrics to define:
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Customer Lifetime Value) — must be 3x CAC minimum
- Payback period
- Gross margin
- MRR/ARR growth rate (if subscription)
- Burn rate + runway (if startup)

### Step 5: 12-Month Roadmap
Q1: Foundation (what must exist to start?)
Q2: Traction (first paying customers + proof of concept)
Q3: Scale (what needs to be built for growth?)
Q4: Optimization (what can be improved from data?)

### Step 6: Key Metrics Dashboard
North Star metric (ONE metric that drives everything)
Supporting metrics (3-5 that feed the north star)
Warning metrics (what signals a problem?)

## Output
- Business Model Canvas (completed)
- Competitive positioning statement
- Growth strategy selection + rationale
- Unit economics model
- 12-month roadmap
- Metrics dashboard design

## Quality checks
- [ ] Business model has a clear path to revenue
- [ ] LTV > 3x CAC
- [ ] North star metric is specific and measurable
- [ ] Roadmap is achievable (not fantasy)
- [ ] Risks identified and mitigated
