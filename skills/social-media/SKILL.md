# SKILL: Social Media Strategy

## Purpose
Build a social media presence that creates real business outcomes — not just vanity metrics.
Followers who don't buy are an audience. Followers who do are customers.

## Platform Playbooks

### LinkedIn — B2B, Professional Audience

**Algorithm signal**: Dwell time (how long do people read?), early engagement (first hour matters most)

**Post types that work**:
```
1. Lessons learned posts
   "I [did something for X months]. Here's what I learned:"
   [5-10 numbered lessons]
   Most underrated: [counterintuitive insight]

2. Story + insight
   "[Specific scenario from your experience]"
   [3 paragraphs max]
   "The insight: [one takeaway]"

3. Contrarian take
   "Unpopular opinion: [thing everyone says] is wrong."
   [Evidence or reasoning]
   "What actually works: [your alternative]"

4. Behind the scenes / process
   "Here's how we [accomplished something]:"
   [Step by step, specific numbers]

5. Data + commentary
   "[Interesting stat or finding]"
   "What this means for [audience]:"
   [3 implications]
```

**LinkedIn posting rules**:
```
Frequency: 3-5x per week (consistency > frequency)
Best time: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am local time
Format: No links in post body (add in comments — links kill reach)
First line: must stop the scroll (question, bold claim, or incomplete sentence)
Hashtags: 3-5 relevant, niche-specific (not #business or #marketing)
Engagement: Reply to every comment within 24 hours
Length: Sweet spot is 300-1200 characters (medium length)
```

### Twitter/X — Thought leadership, developers, news

**Algorithm signal**: Replies, retweets with comment, time spent on thread

**Post types that work**:
```
1. Hot take
   "[Bold claim that challenges conventional wisdom]"
   
2. Thread (most powerful format)
   Tweet 1: Hook (promise of value)
   Tweet 2-8: Content (one insight per tweet)
   Final tweet: Summary + CTA
   
3. Question
   "[Specific question your audience is thinking about]"
   Start with a contrarian framing
   
4. Industry observation
   "[What you're noticing that others aren't talking about yet]"

5. Reframe
   "People think [common belief]. But actually [better model]."
```

**Twitter posting rules**:
```
Frequency: 1-3x per day (more for growth phase)
Best time: 8-9am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm (your audience's timezone)
Threads: 5-15 tweets, end with "Follow for more [topic]"
Engagement: Reply to others MORE than you post
Profile: clear bio with who you help + link to main CTA
Pinned tweet: best performing content or current offer
```

### Instagram — Visual brands, D2C, creators

**Algorithm signal**: Save rate (most important), shares, comments, time watched (Reels)

**Content pillars (use 3-4 consistent themes)**:
```
Pillar 1: Education (teach something valuable)
Pillar 2: Inspiration (aspire to their goals)
Pillar 3: Entertainment (make them laugh or feel)
Pillar 4: Behind the scenes (authentic process)
```

**Reels formula (most reach)**:
```
Hook (0-3 seconds): [visual + text overlay that stops scroll]
Problem: "If you've ever [relatable problem]..."
Solution: "[Your approach]"
Result: "And this is what happened..."
CTA: "Save this for next time you [situation]"
```

**Instagram posting rules**:
```
Reels: 3-5x per week for growth
Feed posts: 3-4x per week
Stories: daily (relationship building, not reach)
Best times: 6-9pm local, weekends
Caption: first line hooks, value in body, CTA at end
Hashtags: 5-15 mix of niche (10k-500k posts) — avoid mega tags
Engagement: reply to all comments, engage with similar accounts
```

### TikTok — Entertainment, discovery, Gen Z + millennials

**Algorithm signal**: Completion rate (how many finish the video), shares

**Formula**:
```
0-3 seconds: Hook ("POV:" or "[Bold claim]" or "[Unexpected visual]")
3-30 seconds: Value delivery (fast-paced, visual, direct)
Last 5 seconds: Soft CTA ("Follow for part 2" or just end on insight)
```

**TikTok rules**:
```
Optimal length: 15-60 seconds for discovery, 1-3 minutes for depth
Post: 1-3x per day during growth phase
Trending sounds: use trending audio with original content
Text overlays: required (most watch with sound off)
Captions: 1-3 sentence hook, 3-5 hashtags (mix of niche + trending)
```

### YouTube — Long-form authority, SEO, education

**Algorithm signal**: Click-through rate (thumbnail + title) + watch time percentage

**Video SEO**:
```
Title: [Primary keyword] | [Benefit or Curiosity hook] (60 chars)
Description: first 3 lines are key (include keyword, what the video covers, links)
Tags: 5-10 specific tags (not broad ones)
Thumbnail: face + emotion + text overlay (3-5 words max)
Chapters: add timestamps for every topic (keeps people watching)
Cards + End screens: point to related videos
```

**Video structure**:
```
0-30 seconds: Hook (what they'll learn + proof it's worth their time)
30-60 seconds: Intro (who you are + why you're the one to teach this)
[Main content]: logical chapters with B-roll
Last 2 minutes: Summary + what to watch next + subscribe CTA
```

## Content Calendar System

### Monthly Planning
```
Week 1: [Content pillar 1] × all platforms
Week 2: [Content pillar 2] × all platforms
Week 3: [Repurpose best-performing content]
Week 4: [Product/offer content + community engagement]
```

### Repurposing Matrix
```
Original: Long-form video (YouTube)
→ Twitter/LinkedIn: Key insights as threads
→ Instagram Reels: Best 60 seconds
→ TikTok: Same clip
→ Blog post: Transcript + editing
→ Email newsletter: Key points + link to video
→ Twitter: Quotes from video (10 tweets)
```

### Analytics to Track Weekly
```
Per platform:
  Reach / Impressions
  Engagement rate (engagements / reach × 100%)
  Profile visits
  Link clicks / website traffic

Business metrics:
  Email signups from social
  Demo requests from social
  Revenue attributed to social (UTM parameters)

Engagement rate benchmarks:
  Instagram: 1-3% = average, 3-6% = good, 6%+ = excellent
  LinkedIn: 2-5% = good
  Twitter: 0.5-2% = good (lower due to volume)
  TikTok: 3-9% = good
```

- Repurposing workflow
- Analytics dashboard with benchmarks
